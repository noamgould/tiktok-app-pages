Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This application ("the App") is a personal automation tool built and operated by an individual creator. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

1. Data Collected

The App does not collect, store, or process personal data belonging to any third party. It only accesses the operator's own video files and the operator's own social media account credentials, solely to upload the operator's own content.

2. Data Sharing

No data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party. The App communicates only with the social media platforms it is explicitly authorized to post to (such as TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube), using official APIs.

3. Data Storage

Video files and scheduling information are stored locally on the operator's own device and personal cloud storage. No data is transmitted to or stored on any server operated by a third party other than the social media platforms themselves.

4. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with TikTok, Instagram (Meta), and YouTube (Google) solely to publish the operator's own content via their respective official APIs. Each platform's own privacy policy governs how they handle data once received.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact the operator directly through the platform where this App is registered.
